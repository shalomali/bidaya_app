**Last Updated: 2026-03-17**

This Privacy Policy applies to the Bidaya application (hereby referred to as "Application") for mobile and web devices, created by **ZUniques** (hereby referred to as "Service Provider") as a Free service. This service is intended for use "AS IS".

---

## 1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information to provide and improve its services. This includes:

### User-Provided Information
- **Account Data:** Email address and User ID when you register.
- **Profile Data:** Full name, phone number, education history, work experience, skills, and certifications.
- **Documents:** Uploaded CV/résumé and profile pictures.
- **Startup Data:** Company name, industry, and opportunity details.

### Automatically Collected Information
- Device Internet Protocol (IP) address.
- Operating system and browser type.
- Interaction data (pages visited, time spent, date of visit).
- **Crash Reports:** Technical logs via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

## 2. App Permissions

To function correctly, the Application may request the following permissions:
- **Internet Access:** Required to sync data with Firebase and provide real-time matching.
- **Storage/Photo Library:** Required for users to upload CVs, résumés, and profile images.
- **Notifications:** Required to send alerts regarding application status updates, messages, and task completions.

## 3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to power the Application. These services may collect information used to identify you:
